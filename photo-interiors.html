Background

In 2014, I picked up my first DSLR camera in an introductory class for photography. After the course, I wanted to keep working on my photography and worked hard to develop my own style.

I began by carving out one day a week to take photo walks, mostly around Gastown and Mount Pleasant. I started taking photos of the interior spaces of small boutiques and local shops, and then posting it on social media. I managed to build a strong relationship with many of the shop owners.

Eventually, clients began to approach me to take photos of their interior spaces and I’ve had the opportunity to help craft the art direction and content strategies for local makers and shops. Here’s a collection of some of my favourites.
